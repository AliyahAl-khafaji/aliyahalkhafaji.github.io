Our Story

We started Pacific Trails Resort 30 years ago with a dream and some determination. We have gone through many ups and downs throughout the years but thanks to all of you the resort is flourishing. We envisioned this place as the ultimate getaway from reality and city life, with luxury camping and many activities reconnecting with nature is at the forefront.

Our Mission

Here at Pacific Trails Resort we aim to help you forge a connection with nature while creating lasting memories. We hope that our resort will bring you comfort, relaxation, and the rejuvenation, and a deep sense of community. We have something here for everyone, and we tailor every vacation to your specific needs for ease and peace of mind.
